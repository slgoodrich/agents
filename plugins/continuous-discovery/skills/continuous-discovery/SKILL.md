# Continuous Discovery

## Overview
Weekly touchpoints with customers to continuously discover opportunities, validate assumptions, and make better product decisions.

## Core Principles
1. **Weekly customer contact** - At minimum
2. **Cross-functional participation** - Product trio (PM, Designer, Engineer)
3. **Focus on outcomes** - Not outputs
4. **Continuous learning** - Build knowledge over time

## Opportunity Solution Tree

### Outcome (Top)
Desired business or customer outcome

### Opportunities (Middle)
Customer needs, pain points, desires

### Solutions (Lower)
Ideas for addressing opportunities

### Experiments (Bottom)
Tests to evaluate solutions

## Discovery Activities

### Generative Research
- Customer interviews
- Contextual inquiry
- Observation
- Journey mapping

### Evaluative Research
- Usability testing
- Prototype testing
- Assumption testing
- A/B testing

## Weekly Cadence
- **Monday**: Review last week's learnings
- **During week**: 3-5 customer interviews
- **Friday**: Synthesis and opportunity mapping

## Assumption Testing
1. **Identify assumptions** - What must be true?
2. **Assess risk** - What's impact if wrong?
3. **Design tests** - How to validate cheaply?
4. **Run experiments** - Learn and iterate

## When to Use
Ongoing product development, feature validation, market exploration, customer-centric culture
