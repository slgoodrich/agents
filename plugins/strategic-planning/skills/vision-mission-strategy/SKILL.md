# Vision, Mission & Strategy

## Vision
Aspirational future state (3-5 years)
- Inspiring and ambitious
- Customer-focused
- Clear direction

## Mission
Purpose and reason for existence
- Why we exist
- Who we serve
- What we do

## Strategy
How we achieve vision
- Market positioning
- Competitive advantage
- Key initiatives

## When to Use
Product launches, strategic planning, team alignment
