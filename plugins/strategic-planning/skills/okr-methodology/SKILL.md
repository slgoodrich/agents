# OKR Methodology

## Overview
Objectives and Key Results framework for goal-setting and execution.

## Structure
**Objective**: Qualitative, inspirational goal
**Key Results**: 3-5 measurable outcomes

## Best Practices
- Objectives are aspirational
- Key Results are measurable
- 60-70% achievement = success (stretch goals)
- Quarterly cycles
- Transparent across organization
- Regular check-ins

## Cascading
Company OKRs → Team OKRs → Individual OKRs

## Tracking
- Weekly progress updates
- Scoring (0.0-1.0 scale)
- Retrospective and learning

## When to Use
Quarterly planning, goal alignment, strategy execution
